# Solutions

The running times are...

* Selection sort: O(n<sup>2</sup>)

* Insertion sort: O(n<sup>2</sup>)

* Linear search: O(n)

* Binary search: O(lg n)

* Random index: O(1)

* Finding duplicates: O(n<sup>2</sup>)

* Finding sum of triplets: O(n<sup>3</sup>)

* Fibonacci sequence: O(2<sup>n</sup>)
